Privacy Policy for Cardly Connect
Cardly Connect Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Cardly Connect allows users to create accounts and send digital cards to recipients by email.

Information We Collect:

* Email addresses provided during account creation.
* Account information necessary for app functionality.
* Recipient email addresses entered by users.
* Card messages and sender information entered by users.

How Information Is Used:

* To create and manage user accounts.
* To send digital cards to recipients.
* To provide and improve app functionality.

Data Storage: User account information, including login credentials and card history, is stored locally on the user's device to support account functionality and saved cards. Accounts can be deleted permanently.  

Third-Party Services:
Cardly Connect uses third-party email delivery services to transmit digital cards to recipients.

Accounts can be deleted permanently
